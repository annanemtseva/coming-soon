Eduardo Akhramovych

Art Nation General Producer. Serial entrepreneur.

Eduardo has been working in the content market for about 30 years. Founded several businesses: audio-visual production and distribution company, the largest producer of karaoke products in the CIS market, a film distributor, one of top-5 YouTube media networks, the first brand licensing agency in Ukraine etc. Worked as the General Producer/Director of Ukrainian State animation studio "Ukrainimafilm".

Eduardo’s producer portfolio includes more than 50 animated episodes, including "Babai" (2014), the first Ukrainian animated feature film. Since 2011, he has been producing brands. Producer of brands GAPCHINSKA, The Goose, Prima Maria, Kozaky and others.

In 2017-2018 he initiated creation of books with augmented reality (AR): more than 877,000 copies sold. Eduardo produces variety of projects: brands; loyalty programs; animated films; goods with augmented reality (AR) etc.
